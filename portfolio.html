CHAMPION TROPHIES & AWARDS

Website | Instagram

Champion Trophies & Awards is an engraving and awards business that has operated out of Morgantown, WV for many decades.

After never having a brand, website, or social media, the time finally came to give this business an ownable visual identity and a web presence. Our solution was a brand that reflected the elegance and age of the business, using typographic flourishes, and low-key photography that highlights the beautiful gold awards.

BURNS SCALO REAL ESTATE

Website | Instagram

Greenmont is a historical district in Morgantown that contains some of the city's oldest structures. Adelheid, a landlord in the area wanted a website to showcase how great the area is, all of its perks and anemities, as well as show off some of her properties.

This project required a lot of guidance in terms of choosing a central goal, as there was a ton of content to be included. The website was to be as much about the community and its rich history as it was rentals, and the story of how the rentals were remodeled by Adelheid and her family.

Home Brewed Photography

Home Brewed Photography is one of my best friend's photography business. She came to me to design a logo, which was eventually expanded into a full brand including social media graphics and custom website — all designed in-house.

Nada and I were long-time co-workers at a cafe in Morgantown, both caffeine addicts, so when it came to metaphors she quickly jumped to coffee. We took that concept and ran with it. The camera logo is constructed of a coffee cup with latte art, and each icon has a coffee-related reference while still remaining relevant to photography.

MORGANTOWN LAND REUSE AND PRESERVATION AGENCY

Morgantown Land Reuse and Preservation Agency is a city organization whose purpose is to repair, restore, and maintain historic properties in the Morgantown area.

This logo is currently used on the Morgantown city website as well as official documents for the organization. It will eventually be used widely for signs, and hopefully be expanded into a full brand once the pandemic ends and the committee can reconvene.

ROLLING HILLS ROLLED ICE CREAM

Rolling Hills Rolled Ice Cream is an up and coming shop in Morgantown, WV that we designed the logo and brand for. Starting a business during the COVID-19 pandemic, and a time when polarizing politics dominated the headlines, our client wanted to brand his business as a place for inclusivity and kindness. This led to the creation of the heart designs, as a part of a "kindess campign."

Check back for updates on this project as this shop plans to have its grand-opening in the spring of 2021.
